Use It

  1. Serve this folder on your laptop, open it on the phone, then tap Start phone sensors.
  2. Stand at the doorway, phone vertical at chest height, point the top edge toward the far wall, then tap Set origin.
  3. For a room scan, walk the perimeter slowly, pause at each corner, and tap Mark corner. Walk a lawnmower path through the room and tap Add RF sample at grid points.
  4. For person movement, place the phone fixed on a wall shelf or tripod, screen facing the room. Real movement needs an external CSI/BFI/RTT/RSSI feed. Phone-only mode only detects the phone moving.
  5. Connect ESP32/Nexmon/PicoScenes/Wi-BFI output through the WebSocket schema to fill the heatmap and target markers.
